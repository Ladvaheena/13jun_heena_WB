Qu:4 What are HTML Entities? With Example.

Answer: A html entities is a piece of text that begins with an Ampersand(&) and ends with a Semicolon(;).
↣ Entities are frequently used to display special charactersin web page or web site. 
Example:
        ↣ &(nbsp); used for give space in content part
        ↣ &(lt); used for lessthan(<) symbol.
        ↣ &(gt);used for greaterthan(>) symbol.
        ↣ &(emp);used for (&)symbol.
        ↣ &(copy);used for copyright(©) symbol.etc..
