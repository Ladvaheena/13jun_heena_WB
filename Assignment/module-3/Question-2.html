Qu:-2 How to embed audio and video in a webpage?

Answer:
↦ With use of "audio" and "video" tags of HTML5,we can embed audio and video in webpage.
↦ In both types of tags the path of the audio & video file pass into src attribute of the source tag.
↦ There are some other property,which are same in this tags,likes
  1. autoplay:-------used to start audio & video automatically.
  2. muted:--------used to mute(no sound) the audio & video in web pages.
  3. loop:--------used to continuously play audio & vedio.
1.Audio
↦ With use of audio tag of HTML-5 ,we can apply audio to the web-page.
↦ Controls must be write in audio tag.
↦ Syntax: 
        <audio controls >
           <source src="path of audio file">
        </audio>

↦ Controls must be write in audio tag.
↦ In video tag, we can also add "poster",which is show on video until start the video. It is appild in poster attribute in video tag.
↦ Syntax: 
            <video controls >
               <source src="path of video file">
