What are all the types of Pop up boxes available in JavaScript?

Answer:
->There are three types of pop-up boxes available in JavaScript.
->All pop-boxes apper,when web-pages or web-site is loaded.
  1. Alert-box
  2. Prompt-box
  3. Confirm-box

1.Alert-box

-> This box used to give alert,when run the webpages or website.
-> If user click on "OK" then, the webpages or website will run.
->Syntax:alert('write any statement');
->One example of it given here,
        <script>
        alert('are you sure to show this');
        </script>

2.Prompt-box


-> This box is used to write any information from user.
->Syntax:prompt('write any statement');
->One example of it given here,
        <script>
        prompt('write your name');
        </script>

3.Confirm box


-> This box is used to take confirmation of any statements or logics from the user.
->Syntax:confirm('write any statement');
->One example of it given here,
        <script>
        confirm('are you confirm to show this page');
        </script>
