Qu:-18 What are the media types allowed by CSS?

Answer:
↦ There are various types of media,which are allowed by CSS.
↦ List of it given below,
  1. All Device-----------used to all device.
  2. Printer
  3. Projection
  4. Screen-------------used for all kind is screen like,computer,tablets & smart-fons etc..
  5. Speech----------used for screenreaders.
↦ Media type applied in "media attribute" in link tag in head tag.
↦ Syntax: 
       <head>
       <link ref="stylesheet" href="#" media="type of media" >
       </head >
